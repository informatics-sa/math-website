What are Math Olympiads?

Math olympiads are competitive mathematical competitions for students under the age of 20. A competition consists of challenging mathematical problems that require creative problem-solving, logical reasoning, and deep mathematical understanding.

This intellectual sport relies on analytical thinking, mathematical creativity, and proof-writing skills to tackle complex problems within a limited time frame.

Why Math Team?

The Saudi Math Team includes the strongest mathematical talents in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Team members are selected through a rigorous selection process that includes competitions and intensive mathematical training.

The team represents the Kingdom in the International Mathematical Olympiad (IMO) and regional competitions, and has achieved many accomplishments at the global level.

How to Join the Team?

You can join the Saudi Math Team through:

  • Participating in national mathematics competitions and achieving excellent results
  • Demonstrating exceptional mathematical talent and problem-solving abilities
  • Completing intensive training programs and qualifying examinations
  • Showing dedication to mathematical excellence and continuous learning
